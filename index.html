About me

Honorable Cyril James Omini is a distinguished leader and exemplary statesman, serving as the Honorable Member representing Yakurr 1 State Constituency in the Cross River State House of Assembly. With a sterling reputation for excellence, Hon. Omini is currently the Chairman of the House Committee on Finance, where he oversees critical financial policies and decisions that shape the economic trajectory of cross river state.

As a seasoned politician, Hon. Cyril James Omini has consistently demonstrated visionary leadership and a deep commitment to the development of his constituency and Cross River State at large. His extensive experience in public service is underscored by a passion for good governance, transparency, and accountability.

A renowned philanthropist, Hon. Omini has touched countless lives through his selfless acts of generosity and community-driven initiatives. His philanthropic endeavors span various sectors, including education, healthcare, and economic empowerment, with a particular focus on uplifting the underprivileged and marginalized members of society.
